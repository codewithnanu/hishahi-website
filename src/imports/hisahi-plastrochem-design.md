Design a modern, premium, responsive B2B manufacturing + e-commerce website for a plastic packaging company called Hisahi Plastrochem.

The website should combine the professional feel of a manufacturing website (like Siemens or BASF) with the product browsing experience of Myntra or Amazon, where customers can view container details and add products to cart.

The brand message is “Packaging That Performs.”

The UI should feel clean, modern, trustworthy, and industrial-tech focused.
